work with father

A great deal of my current working practice is in co-creation with my Dad, constructing live performative based works, often with an accidental audience. This partnership allows me to adopt a playful, authentic insight into learning and teaching, within close family relationships, through everyday behavioural action. I considered the roles of participator/collaborator as well as taking into account authorship and agency of a piece of co-created work.

In Simpson’s, she’s set up a two row cinema, and is showing Westerns at specific times throughout the show, as a means for discussing the different issues, representations and roles at play in the films. - The Skinny
